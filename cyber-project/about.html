About the Presidential Communication Unit

The Presidential Cyber Unit (PCU) is a critical infrastructure responsible for managing and strategizing communication efforts for the highest levels of government. The Presidential Cyber Unit is a specialized team established to enhance the government's digital presence, ensure cybersecurity, and effectively communicate government initiatives to the public. In the context of the Second Republic, the unit plays a pivotal role in articulating government programs, showcasing projects, and fostering public engagement through digital platforms

Our Core Responsibilities

  • Strategic Communication Management

    • Craft clear, consistent, and compelling narratives around government programs to ensure public understanding and support. - Highlight the benefits and impact of these programs on citizens' lives.

  • Public Information Dissemination
  • Media Engagement and Relations
  • Digital and Traditional Media Coordination
