GreenThumb Community Gardens Site Plan

Site Name

GreenThumb Community Gardens

This name represents the gardening focus (GreenThumb) while emphasizing community collaboration. The name is memorable and clearly communicates the site's purpose.

Optional domain availability: greenthumb-gardens.org

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a central hub for local gardening initiatives, providing:

  • Profiles of community gardens with locations and contact information
  • Calendar of gardening workshops and volunteer opportunities
  • Educational resources on sustainable gardening practices
  • Volunteer/donation forms and garden registration system

Scenarios

  • Where is the nearest community garden to my location?
  • How can I sign up for the upcoming composting workshop?
