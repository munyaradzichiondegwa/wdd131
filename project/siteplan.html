Site Plan for Local Community Gardening Initiatives

Site Name

The Garden Community Hub

This name reflects the website's purpose to connect community gardening efforts and resources, fostering a sense of community and collaboration among gardening enthusiasts.

Optional domain availability: thegardencollective.org

Site Purpose

The site aims to serve as a comprehensive hub for information on local community gardening initiatives. It will provide resources and information to engage users in gardening, highlighting the benefits of community involvement in green spaces.

Scenarios

  • What community gardens are available in my area?
  • How can I get involved or volunteer for gardening events?

Color Schema

Color 1: Dark Green (#2d5a27) - Used for headings and important highlights.

Color 2: Orange (#e57f45) - Used for sub-headings and buttons.

Color 3: Light Beige (#f5e6d3) - Used for backgrounds of sections and cards.

Typography

Font 1: Poppins - used for all body text and headings.

Font 2: Merriweather - used for special emphasis or quotes.
