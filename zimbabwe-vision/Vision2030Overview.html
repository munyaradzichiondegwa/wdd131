Vision and Mission

Zimbabwe Vision 2030 aims to transform the country into an upper middle-income economy by fostering inclusive growth, innovation, and resilience.

Strategic Pillars

  • Governance and Peace
  • Economic Stability
  • Social Cohesion
  • Environmental Sustainability

Program Continuum

Transitional Stabilisation Programme (TSP)

The foundation for NDS1, focusing on fiscal discipline and economic restructuring (2018–2020).

National Development Strategy 1 (NDS1)

Achievements include GDP growth, infrastructure improvements, and SME development (2021–2025).

National Development Strategy 2 (NDS2)

Set to achieve 7% GDP growth, 2M jobs, and thematic clusters for economic expansion (2026–2030).
